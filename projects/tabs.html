A tab managment system for chrome to work side by side with bookmarks. Store websites into a divider to cut down on heavy tab clutter and heavy lag.

Chrome Store: Unreleased

Source: github.com/BrandonColbert/Tabs



Create a divider using the + icon.





Compress a tab into a divider by clicking the buble.





Afterwards, you can re-open your tabs using the divider.





For more options, the divider's dedicated page can be opened by right-clicking the bubble.





View and navigate sites without opening a new tab.





Use the searchbar to home in on the sites you're looking for.
